Know Your Limits

Setting personal gaming limits is one of the most effective ways to maintain control over your gaming activity. A responsible player:

  • Sets a budget they can afford to lose as entertainment
  • Never gambles with money needed for essentials like rent, food, or utilities
  • Establishes both deposit limits and time limits
  • Monitors their gaming frequency and spending
  • Takes regular breaks from gaming
  • Seeks support if gaming becomes problematic

At Matrix Spins Casino, you can access comprehensive limit-setting tools directly from your Account Settings. These include deposit limits, loss limits, and session time limits. We encourage all players to use these features to create a gaming experience that fits within their budget and lifestyle.

Self-Assessment

Problem gambling can develop gradually. It's important to recognize warning signs early. If you answer yes to any of these questions, you may want to consider reducing your gaming activity or seeking support:

Warning Signs of Problem Gambling

  • Chasing Losses — Continuing to gamble to win back money you've lost
  • Gambling with Money You Can't Afford to Lose — Using money intended for bills, rent, food, or other essentials
  • Neglecting Responsibilities — Spending so much time gaming that work, school, family, or personal care suffer
  • Borrowing to Gamble — Taking out loans, using credit cards, or asking others for money to fund gaming
  • Lying About Gaming — Hiding the amount of time spent gaming or concealing losses from family or friends
  • Tolerance and Escalation — Needing to gamble with larger amounts to achieve the same excitement
  • Failed Control Attempts — Repeatedly trying and failing to reduce gaming activity
  • Emotional Escape — Gaming primarily to cope with stress, anxiety, or depression

If you recognize these signs in your own behavior, we strongly encourage you to reach out to one of our support resources listed below.
